Households where the “number of children treated” the team typed at the top of the form doesn’t match the number of treated child rows in the repeat group below. A consistent gap suggests under-filled forms - repeat-group rows being skipped after the summary count is entered.

After-Hours Submissions
Forms started outside 6:00 am – 7:00 pm local time - possible data back-entry or device clock error

Very Fast Forms (<3 min)
Completed in under 3 minutes - a full household visit cannot credibly be done that fast; strong signal of skipped questions or rushed entry
-
Very Slow Forms (>60 min)

GPS coordinates fall outside the boundary of the LGA the team reported being in
-
Stacked GPS Points
Two or more forms that share the exact same GPS coordinates - a red flag for repeat submissions from a single spot (e.g. a worker sitting at the LGA HQ and generating forms rather than visiting households).

% of households that declined treatment - social mobilisation may be needed in high-refusal LGAs

Settlement Coverage Breakdown
Per-LGA picture of where the campaign still needs to go. Each settlement falls into one of three buckets:
  • Not visited - no GPS point has ever been logged inside this settlement. The team hasn't physically reached it yet.
  • Partially covered - at least one form was submitted from inside, but fewer than 70 % of the settlement's grid cells contain a GPS point. The team reached part of it; a mop-up pass is needed.
  • Fully covered - ≥ 70 % of the grid cells have a GPS point inside, OR (when no grid is loaded) any GPS point falls inside the settlement polygon. This matches the green shading on the map.
Covered % is fully-covered settlements ÷ total settlements for the LGA - the share of settlements that have crossed the “done” threshold.

Terms of Use & Data Privacy Notice
SARMAAN MDA Monitoring Dashboard · Sokoto State

About this Dashboard

This dashboard presents real-time monitoring information from the SARMAAN-supported Mass Drug Administration (MDA) programme, for the purpose of campaign oversight, coverage tracking, and data quality assurance.

Purpose & Limitation of Use

  • The figures shown are for monitoring purposes only and may change as field data is cleaned, validated, and reconciled.
  • This dashboard is not the official source of record. Final coverage and treatment figures are released through the appropriate programme channels after data cleanup is completed.
  • Information may not be used to make individual clinical, legal, or commercial decisions.

Data Privacy

  • No personally identifying information of treated individuals is displayed. All published figures are aggregated to the settlement, ward, or LGA level.
  • Geographic coordinates shown are tied to administrative boundaries and team activity, not to identifiable persons or households.
  • The platform processes personal data of authorised users (e.g. usernames, role, login activity) solely for access control and audit, in line with applicable Nigerian data-protection guidelines including the Nigeria Data Protection Act, 2023.

Acceptable Use

  • You agree to use the dashboard only for legitimate campaign oversight, public health, and research purposes.
  • You will not attempt to deanonymise data, scrape it at scale, share access credentials, or interfere with the platform's operation.
  • Where you re-share screenshots or extracts, please clearly mark the figures as preliminary, monitoring data subject to change.

By clicking Accept & Continue, you confirm that you have read and agree to these terms.

Your acceptance is remembered on this device.
