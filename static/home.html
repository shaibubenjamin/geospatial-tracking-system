About Us
ERITAS — SARMAAN MDA

ERITAS — Evidence through Real-time Intelligence, Tracking, and Accountability Systems — is the monitoring platform built for SARMAAN MDA, the Mass Drug Administration programme. It turns field data into real-time, accountable evidence that programme managers can act on.

Mass Drug Administration (MDA) delivers preventive treatment to eligible children across whole communities, round after round. During each round, field teams move from settlement to settlement recording every child treated — and ERITAS tracks that effort live, measuring coverage against baseline targets, flagging data-quality issues, and surfacing team performance as the data arrives from the field.

ERITAS unifies the collection tools the programme already uses — CommCare and others — into one place, validating and visualising incoming data continuously. Each state's rounds run side by side, scoped by access so every user only ever sees the data they are permitted to.

Platform Capabilities
Multi-source data integration
Bring CommCare, Kobo Toolbox, ODK Central, Google Drive and flat files together through one common connection layer.
Real-time coverage monitoring
Track submissions, served counts and coverage % by LGA and ward against baseline targets, updated as data arrives.
Automated data-quality checks
GPS validation, duplicate detection, after-hours flagging and form-duration anomaly detection, surfaced per team.
Geographic boundary drill-down
Interactive map from State → LGA → Ward → Settlement with live visitation overlays.
Team movement & performance
Movement paths, daily productivity and per-team quality flags to see who is working where.
Role & area-scoped access
State- and LGA-scoped accounts so every user only ever sees the data they are permitted to, on web and mobile.
Cross-round trends & comparison
Compare rounds side by side on coverage, refusals and data quality to see what is improving over time.
Public & shareable dashboards
Opt-in public views per state and round; everything else stays gated behind login.
Secure, auto-expiring sessions
Encrypted source credentials and a 10-minute idle auto-logout, on web and mobile.
