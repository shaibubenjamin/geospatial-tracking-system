About SARMAAN MDA

SARMAAN — Sokoto State Annual Routine Mass Administration of Anthelmintics and Azithromycin — is Sokoto State's flagship preventive health campaign targeting children aged 1 to 59 months across all 23 Local Government Areas.

The campaign delivers Mass Drug Administration (MDA), a WHO-endorsed strategy for the control of Neglected Tropical Diseases (NTDs) and childhood mortality through the annual distribution of anthelmintic drugs and azithromycin to eligible children at the community level.

Field teams conduct household visits and record treatment data digitally via ODK-based mobile forms. This platform aggregates, validates, and visualises that data in real time to support programme managers in tracking coverage against the WHO administrative coverage threshold of 80%.

Medicines Administered
Albendazole (Anthelmintic)
Controls soil-transmitted helminths (STH) and intestinal worms. Single-dose, given annually. Children 12–59 months receive 400 mg.
Azithromycin (Antibiotic)
Targets trachoma and childhood pneumonia mortality. Part of the SAFE strategy. Weight-based dosing for children 1–59 months.
Vitamin A Supplementation
Co-administered where indicated. Reduces child mortality and prevents vitamin A deficiency disorders.
Platform Capabilities
Real-time Coverage Monitoring
Track household forms, children treated, and coverage percentages by LGA and ward against baseline targets.
Automated Quality Checks
GPS validation, duplicate detection, after-hours flagging, and form-duration anomaly detection across all submissions.
Geographic Boundary Drill-down
Interactive MapLibre-powered map with LGA → Ward → Settlement navigation and visitation status overlays.
Team Performance Analytics
Monitor productivity, identify low-performing teams, and track daily forms per distributor against campaign targets.
Campaign Objectives
≥ 80% Administrative Coverage
WHO benchmark across all 23 LGAs; verified against pre-campaign baseline enumeration data.
NTD Elimination (SAFE Strategy)
Contributing to Nigeria's trachoma elimination roadmap through sustained annual rounds of azithromycin mass treatment.
Zero Missed Settlements
Geo-validated settlement-level visitation tracking to ensure no community is left without access to treatment.
Real-time Data Quality Assurance
Automated QC flags enable programme managers to identify and correct field data issues during the campaign window.
